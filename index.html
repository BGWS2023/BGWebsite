Our Vision

To economically advance African-Americans in society

Misson Statement

Our mission is to financially educate and professionally develop the next generation of black men that will inspire others, create sustainable change, and build generational wealth for themselves, their families, and their communities.

Building Generational Wealth and Success, Inc. was created because many individuals aren’t equipped with essential money management or professional development skills. Year after year, young Black adolescents enter the workforce devoid of proper role models in their lives to direct their path and with poor spending practices- we seek to change that.

What We Do

BGWS was made to counteract the challenges and stressors that Black youth face throughout their teenage years to propel them to success as they continue through life. We aim to create sustainable change in low socioeconomic communities of color by adding professional value to our members and working with our community members to create a better and brighter future.

At our core, we seek to advance the Black man in society. Building Generational Wealth and Success is an organization that provides hope and opportunity to black adolescents by teaching them how to become financially literate, possess professional development skills, and enter any space with a “growth & success” mindset!

FAQ

How Do You Build Generational Wealth?

Generational wealth can refer to any kind of asset that a family passes down to children or grandchildren. That wealth can come in many forms — including cash, stock shares, bonds, investment funds, properties, or even shares from a family business.

Why Do We Need Generational Wealth?
Simply put, generational wealth is all about empowering the next generation of your family with financial freedom. If you’re starting from scratch financially or you’ve got a large debt right out of college, you’ll definitely understand the importance of generational wealth first-hand. Think about it: if a child’s parents or loved ones are able to do something like fund their college education, it can make a world of difference to their financial future. Instead of spending decades repaying hugs students loans, they can focus on saving money to buy their first home, take a trip around the world, build start-up funds for a new business, save for their future retirement, or anything in between.
